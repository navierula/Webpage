On Attractiveness
In August 2016, I went around Central Park and asked people: "What is the most attractive thing you find about yourself and why?" I loved all the answers I received, but I compiled the best ones in a short audio clip. I used Adobe Audition to edit my clips. If you'd like to check it out, follow along with my transcript below!

HOST INTRO:
It’s often said that beauty is in the eyes of the beholder, but the best judgment of yourself may very well come from you. Navie Narula asks folks around Central Park what they find most attractive about themselves and why.

LIV – BOSTON, MA
I think the most attractive thing I find about myself is my smile. (0:03)

PARIS – UNITED KINGDOM
(Laughs) My height. (0:01)

EMMA – ANN ARBOR, MI
My eyes ‘cause they’re deep blue like my dad’s (0:03)

WHITNEY - CALIFORNIA
My ambition…and I say that because I’m willing to go great lengths to get the success that I need to go where I want to go in life. (0:08)

JUSTIN - CONNECTICUT
My eagerness to live life everyday. Take time out from work where I’m at out right now in Central Park and balance out life and work. (0:08)

SOLESTINA – BROOKLYN, NY
I think I’m happy to be aware of things. (0:04)

JT – MARYLAND
I think my level-headedness because it keeps me grounded and keeps everyone else around me grounded as well and it kind of dissolves any situations that may be bad. (0:09)

JOCELYN - MEXICO
I think the most attractive thing I find about myself is…smile because that’s the first thing people usually look at. (0:06)

JEHOVAH-JESUS – FORT MYERS, FL
Always say pretty be a good Lord Jesus Christ…and he likes pretty people, so I do what I can to do pretty as much as I could. (0:08)

Thank you for listening. This was Navie Narula, Columbia Radio News.
